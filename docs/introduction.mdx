## Welcome to Dysh API

Dysh is an AI-powered recipe generation platform that creates personalized, location-based meals tailored to your dietary preferences and cooking style. Our API enables developers to integrate intelligent recipe generation into their applications.

Each API call generates **3 different meals** with structured data including:
- Meal names and cooking times
- Calorie information and ratings
- Detailed ingredient lists with measurements
- Step-by-step cooking instructions
- Professional cooking tips and tricks

- **Ingredient Intelligence**: AI converts ingredients to match dietary restrictions
- **Serving Scaling**: Recipes automatically scaled for 2, 4, or 6 people

## Example Response

Here's what a typical recipe generation response looks like:

```json
{
  "meals": [
    {
      "name": "Spaghetti al Pomodoro e Basilico",
      "estimatedCookTime": "25 minutes",
      "calories": "450 calories per serving",
      "rating": 4.6,
      "ingredients": [
        "400g spaghetti",
        "800g ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped",
        "4 cloves garlic, minced",
        "1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped",
        "1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil",
        "100g Parmesan cheese, grated"
      ],
      "instructions": [
        "Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente",
        "Heat olive oil in large pan, add garlic and cook until fragrant",
        "Add tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes",
        "Toss pasta with sauce, add basil and parmesan",
        "Serve immediately with extra cheese"
      ],
      "proTips": [
        "Use San Marzano tomatoes for authentic Italian flavor",
        "Save pasta water to adjust sauce consistency",
        "Add basil at the end to preserve its fresh flavor"
      ]
    }
    // ... 2 more meals
  ],
  "location": "Italy",
  "provider": "gemini"
}
```
